J-Rock

Pretender- Official Dism

Pretender by Official HIGE DANDism is a captivating J-Pop track that has made a significant impact on the Japanese music scene and beyond. Released on April 17, 2019, the song showcases the band's signature style, blending elements of pop and rock with emotionally resonant lyrics.

RADWIMPS - Zenzenzense (movie ver.)

A rock band named RADWIMPS produced this song, which became very popular and became the opening song for the anime movie 'Your Name'. It has a total of 306 million views on youtube and keeps on growing bigger. Both the music video and the movie was very popular.

Zankyosanka - Aimer

Aimer, one of the more popular singers in japan, singing many different anime songs and this song isn't an exception. Zankyosanka is the main theme song for the 2nd season of the anime 'Demon Slayer'. It easily reached 195 million with just over a year and will more than likely keep increasing. The fans of the anime dearly loved both the anime and the song.

K-ROCK:

Rooftop- N.Flying

Rooftop catapulted N.Flying to the forefront of the K-rock scene with its infectious energy and distinctive sound. The track features a compelling mix of rock instrumentation, highlighted by vibrant guitar riffs and a driving rhythm section. Rooftop not only marked a breakthrough for N. Flying but also contributed to the revitalization of rock music in the Korean music industry.

Death Day - Xdinary Heroes

While Xdinary Heroes just debuted during 2022, they had debuted with an amazing song. The song had won an award and brought them many fans.

You were beautiful-Day6

You Were Beautiful" is a standout track from DAY6, a South Korean band known for their emotive lyrics and rock-infused sound. The song showcases DAY6's ability to blend rock elements seamlessly with heartfelt storytelling. With its melancholic yet beautiful melody, "You Were Beautiful" captures the essence of nostalgia and heartbreak.
