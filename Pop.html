JPOP:

Hana - Fuiji Kaze

Made by an upcoming musician, Fuiji Kaze, topping 10th place in this week November Japan Bilboard Chart. He gained popularity in Japan with his song Kirari, 1 year later, his song named Shinunoga E-wa become a trending sound on tiktok, thus making his music known worldwide.

DAOKO × Kenshi Yonezu

This is ranked the #2 most viewed music videos by japanese artists. It has a collective 582 million views with an average of 100k views daily. It is an opening song written, sang and played for the anime movie "Firewords", also knows as Fireworks, Should We See It from the Side or the Bottom? It was released in 2017 and while the movie wasn't very popular with the public, the music absolutely stunned the audience. The song was written by DAOKO and Kenshi Yonezu. It became so popular that singers sang that song and released it for their fans to enjoy the song with their voice.

Stay with Me - Miki Matsubara

In the year 2021, Japanese city pop music starts to gets popular in the internet, with Stay with me by Miki Matsubara being one of them. Other notable, japanese city pops songs that blew up at the same time are Plastic Love by Mariya Takeuchi and 4:00 am by Taeko Onuki.

KPOP:

K-pop or Korean Pop is the internationally popular, aesthetic-driven, style-bending, trendsetting, music genre of the 21st century.

Super Shy - Newjeans

Debuted just last year, New Jeans become a kpop sensation with their fresh sounds and unique concept. Their comeback song, Super Shy hit 100 millions in a matter of months

Antifragile-Lesserafim

Antifragile by Lesserafim is an addicted song, with it's catchy hook solidify their group sound among the masses, while borrowing sounds from some latin inspired style and mixed it with kpop. It's mixed kpop and latin sound becoming a huge hit among kpop fans.

After Like - Ive

Ive become a huge hit as they consistently released catchy and addicting music as their song After Like become a massive hit among kpop fans! Their visuals and talents will shine through in the mV, capturing many hearts.
