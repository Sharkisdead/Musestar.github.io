Awich who's known for being part of the hip hop scene in Japan collab with their fellow rappers/artists creating a cipher like music, with an addicting beat and smooth flow, their slowly take over the the hip hop in Japan.

Cheating is a Crime-Takayan

Takayan brings a compelling narrative to life through haunting melodies and poignant lyrics. In this track, the artist dives deep into the complexities of relationships, painting a vivid picture of heartbreak, betrayal, and the consequences of infidelity. With the hard heating beat , it become a popular song in the internet furthering is career.

ヒプノシスマイク Division All Stars「ヒプノシスマイク -Division Battle Anthem-＋」

In this adrenaline-fueled anthem, the collective talents of the Division All Stars collide, delivering a high-energy sonic experience that transcends musical boundaries. With razor-sharp verses, explosive beats, and a commanding presence, this track is more than just a song – it's a declaration of dominance. Brace yourself as ヒプノシスマイク Division All Stars takes you on a rap battle journey, where each division's unique flavor converges into a symphony of hip-hop mastery.

K-hip hop

Happen Ending - Epik High

As masters of storytelling through music, Epik High crafts a poignant narrative woven into every note of this compelling song. 'Happen Ending' is a musical masterpiece that delicately explores the complexities of relationships, capturing the bittersweet essence of love and its inevitable struggles.

NCT U 엔시티 유 '일곱 번째 감각 (The 7th Sense)

'The 7th Sense' by NCT 127 is not just an ordinary track; it's an experience. With seamless rap verses, captivating vocals, and an enigmatic atmosphere, the song immerses you in a unique blend of rhythm and emotion. It was unique at the time, and age like wine as time goes by.

All I Wanna Do (Feat. Hoody & 로꼬 Loco)

'All I Wanna Do,' featuring the soulful contributions of Hoody and the charismatic flow of 로꼬 Loco. In this chart-topping collaboration, Jay Park effortlessly fuses R&B and hip-hop, creating a dynamic and irresistible anthem. From the smooth vocals of Hoody to the slick verses delivered by Loco, each element of this track adds to the overall sonic experience.
