Popular Music Genres

Have you ever wondered what the most popular music genre is? Who is the most popular artist of those genres? What is their most popular creations? Well this website provides the answers you need for these questions. But lets start off with other commonly asked questions.

What is music?

Music are vocal or instrumental sounds(or both) combined in such a way as to produce beauty of form, harmony, and expression of emotion

How is music produced?

Music is made or created by using sound from human voices or instruments. Sounds are grouped together to form melody, harmony, and rhythm, in combination together and recorded they become a song that is composed. Songs are then finetuned and edited to perfection.

How long does it take for song production?

If offered a good amount of production time and comprehensive mastering of the music, it should take no more than one week. It is entirely dependent on one's mood. Some have created numerous tunes in a single day, while others have taken months to complete. However, one week is usually plenty.
